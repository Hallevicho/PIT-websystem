SONG BY THE PLATTER
ONLY YOU
The song "Only You (And You Alone)" expresses the depth of the singer's love and how their significant other is the only one who can truly complete them, making the world a brighter and happier place.
"Only You (And You Alone)", often shortened to "Only You," is a classic love song by The Platters. Released in 1955, it features smooth doo-wop vocals and heartfelt lyrics about devotion and destiny.
SONG BY MUNIMUNI
BAWAT PIYESA
"Bawat Piyesa" by Munimuni is a song filled with anxiety about losing a loved one. The lyrics describe cherishing every aspect of the person and the fear of a future where they might not be there. While there's sadness, the song also hints at a quiet strength in facing that possibility.
Munimuni likely wrote "Bawat Piyesa" to capture the complex emotions of fearing to lose someone special. The lyrics explore the vulnerability of love and the anxieties that come with it. It may resonate with listeners who have experienced similar feelings.
